Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track custom events?

You can track custom events using our JavaScript library, mobile SDKs, or by making direct API calls. Visit our Event Tracking guide for detailed instructions.

What's the difference between sessions and users?

A user is a unique individual who visits your site or app, while a session represents a single visit. One user can have multiple sessions over time.

How long is data retained?

We retain your raw event data for 12 months by default. You can adjust this in your privacy settings or export your data for longer retention.
